Biography

Heraldo Rozas received his BS and MS degrees in electrical engineering from the University of Chile. He is currently working toward a Ph.D. degree in industrial and system engineering at the Georgia Institute of Technology. His research interests are in data-driven decision-making under uncertainty and failure prognostics. Specifically, he focuses on developing stochastic programming models that leverage real-time data from sensors and other IoT devices for the joint optimization of condition-based maintenance schedules and spare parts provisioning.
